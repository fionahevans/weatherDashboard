Plant available soil water is modelled using the Ritchie two-layer fallow evaporation model described in Ritchie, J.T. 1972, "Model for predicting evaporation from a row crop with incomplete cover".

The break of season is assumed to occur if there is 25mm of rainfall over three days after 25 April and before 5 June, or if there is 5mm of rainfall over three days after 5 June. Crop evapotranspiration (assuming a wheat crop) is estimated using the FAO crop factor method with coefficeients suited to wheat cropping in WA.

See the DPIRD Soil water tool for more information and additional options for changing the break of season estimation and length of growth stages and crop coefficients to allow for different crop types.
