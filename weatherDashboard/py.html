Potential yield is calculated using the French & Schultz model:

Yield (kg/ha) = WUE * (summer rainfall/3 + growing season rainfall - Evaporation)

Values of WUE and evaporation can be changed to best reflect different crop types and locations. For example:

  Wheat Barley Canola Lupins Oats
WUE 20 18 10 12 22
Evap 110 110 110 125 90
